Questions

Good to know before you start.

Do I need prior experience to join? +
No. The course starts from institutional foundations and builds progressively, while 1-on-1 coaching is adapted to wherever you are right now.
What exactly is ICT & Smart Money Concepts? +
A framework for reading how large institutions move price — through liquidity manipulation, market structure, fair value gaps and timing — rather than relying on lagging retail indicators.
Which markets does this apply to? +
The same concepts work across gold (XAU/USD), forex pairs, indices (NAS100, S&P500) and crypto — anywhere there’s enough liquidity for institutional order flow to operate.
How do I get started? +
Message on WhatsApp for 1-on-1 coaching or to enrol in the course. Join the free Discord channel first if you want to see the analysis and community before committing.
